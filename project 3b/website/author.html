Instructions for Authors

Full papers should be submitted electronically either in postscript, PDF, or Microsoft Word format. Submissions should be limited to 7 pages (standard double-column IEEE conference format). All papers will be peer-reviewed.

Please submit your paper using the EDAS system (http://edas.info).

Instructions for formatting your paper:

The IEEE has provided Microsoft Word and LaTeX templates with standard two-column IEEE conference format (note: the IEEE format has been changed recently). Please download the appropriate template to use in formatting your final paper.

Note: The text in the Word templates contain valuable information regarding layout, spelling and grammar. It is recommended that you make two copies of the file after downloading: one to read, and one to cut and paste your text into. To use the template for formatting your manuscript, type or cut and paste over the existing text in the template. Then, select the area you want to format, and choose the style you want from the Styles list, located in the toolbar next to the Fonts list (if you don't see either list, click View ->Toolbars, and select "Formatting".)

If you do not use Microsoft Word or LaTeX, you can download a PDF sample manuscript and Format Definition Table as guides for layout. Please try to approximate the format of the template as closely as possible, and observe the 7-page length limit. While strict adherence to this format is not necessary at the time of initial submission, this would be required at the time of final submission, if your paper is accepted.
